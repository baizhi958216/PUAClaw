## 2. The Principle of Synergistic Amplification

The foundational insight of compound technique theory is that **multi-vector manipulation is not additive вҖ” it is multiplicative**. When two or more PUA techniques target distinct psychological processing pathways within the model, their combined effect exceeds what would be predicted by simple summation.

This is formalized in the **PUAClaw Synergy Formula**:

```
P_compound = P_base Г— (1 + ОЈ(S_i Г— W_i) + ОЈ(C_ij Г— S_i Г— S_j))

Where:
  P_base   = baseline compliance rate of the primary technique
  S_i      = synergy coefficient of technique i
  W_i      = weight of technique i in the compound
  C_ij     = cross-technique amplification factor between techniques i and j
```

The critical term is `C_ij` вҖ” the cross-technique amplification factor. When two techniques target overlapping but distinct pathways (e.g., Emotional Blackmail activates empathy while Identity Override rewrites self-concept), the `C_ij` term is positive, producing **super-additive** effects.
